# Saarthi AI Workforce — MSME Form 

---

## 6. Newness / uniqueness of the innovation

Saarthi delivers rentable, autonomous AI EMPLOYEES for MSMEs — not a chatbot. Each employee runs a real agentic loop (plan → act → observe → reflect → synthesize) that composes specialist skills at runtime and hands back finished, filing-ready work, not advice.

What is new:
1) Persona-scoped agentic engine: the same loop constrained to a job role (job description + SOP + a scoped toolset) so output is professional and on-remit — an AI "employee", not a general bot.
2) Rent-and-integrate model: hire an employee and call ONE REST API from an ERP, cron or Zapier; usage is metered per tenant (agent-as-a-service).
3) Real, deterministic connectors: the official GSTIN check-digit algorithm, Tally-import XML vouchers, and NIC e-way-bill JSON — verifiable artefacts, not hallucinated text.
4) India-MSME-native depth: Udyam, GST/e-invoice, Mudra/PMEGP/CGTMSE, MSMED-Act Samadhaan (delayed payments), FSSAI, BIS, RERA, DPDP, ARAI/FAME — bilingual (Hindi/English), also on Telegram.

No existing product converts generative AI into a rentable, integrable back-office WORKFORCE that produces the finished artefact for the MSME.

---

## 7. Concept & Objective

Concept: India's 63M+ MSMEs run lean and cannot afford specialist staff or rigid enterprise software, so back-office work (finance, GST, compliance, delayed-payment recovery) stalls growth. Saarthi lets any MSME RENT autonomous AI employees — Finance & Schemes, Accounts & GST, Receivables/MSME Samadhaan, Environment, Food Safety, Construction, Electronics, Auto/EV. Hire one, assign a task in plain language, and integrate it via one API call to automate a workflow end to end.

Objectives:
1) Make expert back-office capability affordable and always-on for every MSME.
2) Unlock the finance and schemes MSMEs are owed but never claim (Mudra/PMEGP/CGTMSE).
3) Help MSMEs get paid — the MSMED-Act 45-day rule and Samadhaan, with statutory interest.
4) Automate sector compliance (FSSAI, BIS, RERA, pollution board, DPDP).
5) Deliver finished, filing-ready artefacts (GST invoice to Tally, e-way bill, project report, complaint) — measurable output, not advice.
6) Integrate into existing systems (ERP/cron/Zapier) with metered, agent-as-a-service pricing that scales from one employee to a fleet.

## 8. Potential areas of application in industry / market

Saarthi applies across every Miscellaneous MSME sub-sector plus cross-cutting back-office functions:
- Finance & schemes: loan matching (Mudra/PMEGP/CGTMSE/Stand-Up India), bankable project reports/CMA.
- Accounts & GST: GST invoicing exported to Tally, e-invoice IRN, e-way bills, reconciliation.
- Delayed-payment recovery: MSME Samadhaan filings, Section-16 interest, demand notices, TReDS.
- Environment & Water: CPCB consent (CTE/CTO), EPR, ZLD, BRSR.
- Food/FMCG: FSSAI licensing and compliant labels, HACCP, PMFME subsidy.
- Infrastructure/Construction: Bill of Quantities, RERA, BOCW cess, tenders.
- Electronics/IT/Telecom: BIS-CRS, E-waste EPR, DPDP Act, WPC/ETA, PLI.
- Metals/Auto/EV/Mobility: ARAI/AIS homologation, FAME-II, PLI-Auto, GeM.

Customers: MSME owners; startups; CA and consulting firms (multi-client); incubators/accelerators; industry associations; and government MSME facilitation/Champions centres. Delivery: web app, REST API for ERP/SaaS integration, and Telegram — usable by non-technical, bilingual owners across Bharat.

## 9. Market potential of the idea / innovation

India has 63M+ MSMEs — about 30% of GDP, 45% of exports and 110M+ jobs — a vast, underserved market that cannot afford specialist staff or enterprise software. The AI-employee model is a horizontal, high-margin SaaS.

- TAM: 63M+ MSMEs plus startups, CA/consulting firms, accelerators and government facilitation centres.
- Pricing: per-employee subscription or usage-metered per task (already metered per API key) — low cost to the MSME, near-zero marginal cost to add an employee (a role = job description + functions + toolset).
- Land-and-expand: an MSME hires one employee (e.g. Accounts & GST) then grows the fleet; multi-client CA firms deploy it across many MSMEs.
- Tailwinds: Digital India, Udyam formalisation, GST/e-invoice mandates, ONDC, the MSME credit push, and rapidly cheaper generative AI.
- Moat: real deterministic connectors (Tally/GSTIN/e-way), deep India-compliance coverage, and an integration API. Each new sub-sector is one role definition away, so the product scales across the entire MSME economy with minimal new code.
